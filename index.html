Welcome! This is an online software that allows you to create, modify or visualise a 3d model that can be easily adjusted by changing parameters. To start you may just drag a supported parametric file into the dropping area. Modify the values, press F5 to update the viewer, generate and download your customized item as a 3d printable .stl file. Happy 3D Printing!
